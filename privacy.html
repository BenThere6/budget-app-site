Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Budget App is a personal budgeting tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account and app data: basic account info you provide (if applicable), and your budgeting categories and settings.
  • Financial data via Plaid: if you choose to connect a bank account, Budget App uses Plaid to retrieve account and transaction information.

How we use information

  • To provide budgeting features (transaction import, categorization, reporting).
  • To maintain and improve app reliability and security.

Plaid

Budget App uses Plaid to connect to your financial institution. Budget App does not receive or store your bank login credentials. Plaid may process data according to its own policies.

Data storage and security

We take reasonable measures to protect data. Access tokens and sensitive credentials are stored on the server and are not exposed to the client application.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to connect a bank account and use manual entry (if supported).
  • You can disconnect a bank connection (if supported) to stop future syncing.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: textile-grab-3p@icloud.com
